Lee Haber

While attending a Mandarin lesson at a traditional language school, Lee realized that he wasn't really learning anything or getting better at Mandarin. He also realized that studying on his own only got him so far, that the only way he would become fluent was through practice. He started thinking that there must be a better way to learn a new language. Lee brings with him experience in small business and startups. Lee loves learning languages and has proficiency in Spanish, German, Portuguese and Mandarin.

Chloe MacDonald-Chow

Chloe was born and raised in Vancouver, BC and has a passion for dancing and teaching. She loves to travel and learn about different customs and traditions. She also desires to learn and become fluent in many languages and is currently learning Spanish, Japanese, Chinese, and Korean. Her love for learning has enabled her to understand the many struggles of learning languages and to help students overcome them.

Chloe has 4 years of teaching dance and has completed her TESL Certificate in Vancouver, BC. She will be graduating in December with an Associates of Arts at Douglas College and will transfer to UBC. She aspires to teach at an Elementary and University level and looks forward to teaching English abroad.
