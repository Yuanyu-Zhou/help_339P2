FAQ

Do you offer refunds?

We automatically give refunds if we cancel a program. For individual lessons, we offer a refund if you cancel 3 days before the date of your class. For courses, we offer a refund if you cancel 7 days before the start of the course. We also offer refunds for our Beginner Bootcamps if you completed your homework for 75% of the weeks, attended 75% of the sessions and are not satisfied with your level of the language. If you were not satisfied with the quality of your program, please email us at beyondfluency@gmail.com

What languages do you teach?

Currently, we only teach English and Spanish, however, in the future we would like to teach all languages people want to learn. We believe our method helps students learn or improve their ability in a language better than conventional methods. If you would like us to teach another language, please email us at beyondfluency@gmail.com.

How long are the lessons?

Our lessons are 1.5 hours long. We feel like this the ideal duration for a lesson, both for our students and instructions.

How do I cancel my membership?

Please send an email to beyondfluency@gmail.com or call us at 778-877-8033

How does the homework work for the Beginner Bootcamps?

To learn a language requires doing regular daily practice. It is an essential part of the Beginner Bootcamp experience. To ensure students do the homework, we charge a fine of $50 for every week that the homework goal is not met. This is in addition to the course fees.Students who fall more than 2 weeks behind the course homework target may be removed from the course.
